Recommended User Guidelines

This platform is for people who want intentional, slow, and meaningful dating. It especially welcomes those living in rural or countryside areas, where connection can take time and intention. Everyone is welcome as long as they share the same mindset.

  • Quality over quantity - Fewer choices encourage deeper attention to each person.
  • Honesty and clarity - Be upfront about what you’re looking for and what you’re not.
  • Respect and kindness - Treat every match as a real person, not a swipe.
  • Pace yourself - Slow dating helps you notice compatibility you might otherwise miss.

By following the guidelines above, it will enhance your chances of finding the love of your life. Our goal is to improve yours, and your matches user experience. We don't tolerate any abuse and an account ban will take place if you are disrespectful towards other users.
Safety & Respect Guidelines outlined below:

  • Keep personal information private until trust is built.
  • Report disrespectful or harmful behaviour.
  • No harassment, pressure, or manipulation of any kind.
  • Consent and boundaries are non‑negotiable.
